ABOUT

Many years ago, sometime in the mid-90s, I discovered Klik & Play. That began my delve into video game development. Over the years, I’ve used different game engines like The Games Factory, Clickteam Fusion, and Adventure Game Studio. Nowadays, I mainly use C and Allegro.

I also have a deep love for old computers and ‘90s nostalgia. Listening to and composing midi music, whether it’s with gm.dls sounds or good ole ADLIB/OPL, goes with the territory.

I’m married to the woman of my dreams, and we follow Jesus the best we can.

I started this site to log things that I find interesting because doing them isn’t enough. I have to write about it too.
